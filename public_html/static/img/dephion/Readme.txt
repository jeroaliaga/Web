Dephion Family

In many ways, handmade items fit as collectibles, because they look unique and not boring. Dephion, one of the handmade fonts we made to give a casual and natural impression. We want to get out of the habit of seeing the perfect font type by making the shape imperfect and irregular. Dephion family brings modern calligraphic styles and sans created in a very manual way.

Dephion presents three fonts: regular, slanted and Dephion Sans Desphion script. You can pair them to create a variety of attractive designs with a sense of casual.

Certainly supports multilingual and PUA encoded.
